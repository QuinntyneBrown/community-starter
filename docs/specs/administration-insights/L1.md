# ADMN — Administration and insights

## Product context

Community teams need bounded tools to configure participation and understand outcomes, while
platform and support operators need narrowly scoped operational access. Administration must never
become an unaudited bypass around Community isolation, safety, privacy, billing, or Account security.

## L1-ADMN-001: Bounded Community administration

The platform must let authorized Community roles manage settings, delegated authority, and lifecycle
operations with validation, continuity, impact preview, and server enforcement.
