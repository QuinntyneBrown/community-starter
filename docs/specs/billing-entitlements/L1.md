# BILL — Billing and Entitlements

## Product context

Paid Plans are an optional configuration profile. They are disabled cleanly by default until a
payment provider, merchant model, currencies, tax/invoice responsibilities, refund policy, privacy,
and operational ownership are accepted. When enabled, provider state informs—but never bypasses—
server-owned Subscription and Entitlement rules.

## L1-BILL-001: Safe optional billing boundary

The starter must keep Billing inert by default, validate every enablement prerequisite, and keep
sensitive payment collection within the reviewed provider boundary.
