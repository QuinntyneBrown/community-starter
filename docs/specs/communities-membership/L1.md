# COMM — Communities and membership

## Product context

Accounts organize around distinct Communities. Each Community owns its Memberships, Roles,
Permissions, Spaces, settings, and lifecycle, and the server preserves administrative continuity
and strict multi-community tenancy through every transition.

## L1-COMM-001: Govern Community identity

The platform must let authorized Accounts create, identify, and configure a Community without
breaking stable links or exposing private state.
