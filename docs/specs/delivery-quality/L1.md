# QUAL — Delivery, quality, and operations

## Product context

The starter must make production-scale community behavior reproducible, falsifiable, deployable, and supportable. Quality evidence must match the risk being claimed: an isolated test cannot prove a cross-stack journey, a development server cannot prove routing, and passing builds cannot substitute for operational, recovery, accessibility, privacy, security, or load review.

## L1-QUAL-001: Build layered evidence around product risk

Domain policy, application coordination, UI behavior, provider integration, public contracts, member journeys, and deployed routing must each be verified at the layer capable of exposing their failures.
