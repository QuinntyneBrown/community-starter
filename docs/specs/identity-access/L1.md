# AUTH — Identity and access

## Product context

Accounts need secure, recoverable access across many Communities without allowing credentials,
sessions, Roles, or Permissions from one Community to grant access in another. Authentication and
authorization decisions are server-owned; clients may explain allowed actions but never establish
them.

## L1-AUTH-001: Safe Account lifecycle

The platform must create, verify, recover, deactivate, and securely retire an Account without
disclosing whether an identifier is registered or leaving reusable recovery credentials.
