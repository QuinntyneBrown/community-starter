# SAFE — Moderation, trust, and safety

## Product context

Members need a safe way to report suspected harm, while Moderators need bounded authority, preserved
evidence, consistent policy, and accountable decisions. Safety behavior spans content, Profiles,
Memberships, Messages, Events, discovery, Delivery, appeals, data retention, and emergency response.

## L1-SAFE-001: Safe policy reporting

The platform must publish applicable policy and accept privacy-safe Reports across supported targets
without enabling retaliation, resource discovery, duplicate effects, or reporting abuse.
