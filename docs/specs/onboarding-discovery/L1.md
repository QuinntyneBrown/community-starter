# ONBD — Onboarding and discovery

## Product context

Onboarding moves an eligible Account from first access to a meaningful, understandable Community
experience. Discovery helps the Account choose an eligible Community without exposing private
Communities, blocked relationships, sensitive inference, or fabricated popularity.

## L1-ONBD-001: Complete resumable onboarding

The platform must present, save, validate, skip where optional, and resume onboarding steps while
keeping required eligibility and policy decisions server-owned.
