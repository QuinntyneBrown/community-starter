# OPER — Operations and reliability

## Product context

Members and Community teams need predictable service while Platform Operators need privacy-safe
evidence, owned alerts, repeatable recovery, and bounded failure modes. Production-scale means the
starter defines measurable objectives and proves recovery and capacity; it does not merely contain a
health endpoint or pass a build.

## L1-OPER-001: Operators can understand system and product health

Logs, traces, metrics, health probes, and dashboards correlate requests, Jobs, external calls,
realtime work, and product-defining transitions without exposing sensitive content.
