# STRT — Starter adoption and developer experience

## Product context

Product teams need to clone, understand, specialize, run, verify, and publish the starter without
reverse-engineering hidden workstation state or accidentally shipping sample identities, placeholder
secrets, or starter branding. The starter is a working, traceable baseline rather than an archive of
empty projects or a generator whose output immediately drifts from its source.

## L1-STRT-001: A clean checkout is reproducible

Pinned toolchains, committed dependency resolution, repeatable local services, safe configuration,
and canonical commands take a supported workstation from clone to a verified application.
